About Us

Our History

Established in 1994, National Public School, Gurugram, is a leading CBSE-affiliated school. Over the years, it has grown into a center of academic excellence and holistic development, nurturing students to become responsible and confident citizens.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide a stimulating environment where students learn with joy, curiosity, and discipline. We aim to develop compassionate leaders and lifelong learners who are well-prepared to face global challenges.

Director’s Message

"At National Public School, our focus is not just on education but on character, discipline, and values. We believe that a well-rounded individual makes a better society."

Principal’s Message

"We nurture students in a caring, safe, and stimulating environment. With dedicated staff and a progressive curriculum, we ensure that every child blossoms to their fullest potential."
